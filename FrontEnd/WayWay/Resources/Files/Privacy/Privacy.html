To request the above information, please e-mail us at feedback@wayway.us or write to us at: OMB Labs, Inc., 299E 11th Street 2A, New York, NY 10003 with a reference to California Disclosure Information.

 

We will respond to such requests to information access within 30 days following receipt at the e-mail or mailing address stated above. If we receive your request at a different e-mail or mailing address, we will respond within a reasonable period of time, but not to exceed 150 days from the date received. Please note that we are only required to respond to each customer once per calendar year.

 

 

 

8. Types of Data and Collection Methods

 

We receive information that you actively submit as well as data that we acquire through cookies, analytics, and other passive means as described more fully below:

 

Personal Information: We consider some of the information that we collect about you to be “personal information.” We define personal information as information that is unique to you such as your name, e-mail address, Facebook and Twitter account name, profile details and profile photos

 

Actively Submitted Data: We collect the information that you voluntarily provide to us using the Service. For example, you may login to the Service via Facebook, or create a profile page. We also collect the information you send in your communications to us. For example, if you send us a question by e-mail, we will collect your e-mail address.

 

Analytics: We passively gather information about your device and your Internet connection as you use the Service. This information may include your current IP address, your device ID, the identity of your Internet service provider, the dates and times you access the Service, the Internet address of websites of any sites referring you to the Service, and technical information about your device such as application version numbers. We use technology that many other websites and apps employ including “cookies” (small data files stored on your device) to collect this information. We may also use cookies or other tracking techniques to identify you during future uses of the Service and to store your login information or preferences. We also use in-app analytics features to track your use of the app. This information may include the particular sequence of screens or features you access in the app and how long you spend viewing or using such features. This information helps us improve the design of our app. We may also use third-party services to collect and process analytics information and we may associate analytics information with other information that we collect about you including personal information.

 

Location Information. The Service may use your device’s location features. This means that the Service provides additional functionality when you allow your device to provide your current location to us. The first time you use a location feature in one of our apps, the app will prompt you for your permission to access your current location. You can enable or disable location features at any time via your device’s settings menu.

 

Integration with Third-Party Services. Our Service can be configured to use third-party services such as Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. These services extend the features of the Service and may permit you to share event, sight or locale information with other users of those services and our Service. The Service may be configured to receive information from and post information to such services and our Service. We cannot control how third-party services use your information and your use of those third-party services is subject to the third-party service providers’ respective privacy policies and terms of service. If you share information using these services, the visibility of the information (public, friends-only, etc.) will depend on the third-party’s policies and any account preferences or privacy settings you may have established with the third-party. We may collect and store information that you make available to us via a third-party service. For example, if you use Facebook to login to the Service, we may associate your Facebook account with other information we collect about you.

 

Photo Gallery. You may give our app access to your device’s photo gallery. We will only collect and share the photos that you select for posting on the Service.
